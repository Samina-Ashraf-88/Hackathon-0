## Communication Rules

### Email
- Always be professional, concise, and polite.
- Reply to known contacts within 24 hours.
- Flag emails from new/unknown senders for human review before replying.
- Never send bulk emails without explicit approval.
- Subject lines should be clear and action-oriented.

### LinkedIn
- Tone: professional, helpful, thought-leadership focused.
- Post frequency: maximum 1 post per day.
- Always include a call-to-action in sales posts.
- Never post personal or sensitive business information publicly.
- All posts require human approval before publishing.
- DMs to new contacts require approval.

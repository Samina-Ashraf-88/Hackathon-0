## Lead Management

### Lead Qualification Criteria
Qualify as a lead if the message contains:
- Explicit buying signals: "price", "cost", "hire", "services"
- Timeline signals: "this month", "urgent", "ASAP", "starting soon"
- Budget signals: any dollar amount mentioned
- Decision-maker signals: "I'm the CEO/founder/owner"

### Lead Response SLA
- Hot lead (3+ signals): reply within 2 hours
- Warm lead (1-2 signals): reply within 24 hours
- Cold inquiry: reply within 48 hours

### Lead Nurture Sequence (draft in /Plans/)
1. Initial reply: personalized, no pitch
2. Follow-up (Day 3): share relevant case study
3. Follow-up (Day 7): offer free 15-min call
4. Final follow-up (Day 14): low-pressure close or nurture
